Usage:

Exponentialfunktion: e^(x) or e^x
Sinus: sin(x)
Cosinus: cos(x)
Tangens: tan(x)
Sinc: sinc(x)
Arcsinus: arcsin(x)
Arccosinus: arccos(x)
Arctangens: arctan(x)
Hyperbolic Sinus: sinh(x)
Hyperbolic Cosinus: cosh(x)
Hyperbolic Tangens: tanh(x)
Hyperbolic Arcsinus: arcsinh(x)
Hyperbolic Arccosinus: arccosh(x)
Hyperbolic Arctangens: arctanh(x)
Natural Logarithm: ln(x) or log(x)
Logarithm Base 10: log10(x)
Logarithm Base 2: log2(x)
Factorial: fac(10)
Squareroot: sqrt(x)
PI: pi or PI
